Fifa: 1993 - Onwards

Fifa 94

Fifa is a game about soccer. The player can either play against a computer (CPU)or they can have two to four players playing against each other. The first Fifa game kicked off in 1993. It was called Fifa 94. Every year after, another fifa game would be released around Christmas. Each Fifa name would be one year after the year the game was released(e.g Fifa 12 was released around Christmas 2011).
